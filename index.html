Dr. Wu Lien Teh

the man who invented mask

Here is a timeline of Dr Wu Lien Teh life:

  • 10 Mac 1879-Born in Penang, Malaysia
  • 1896- Pursued his education at Emmanuel College, Cambridge
  • 1903 - returned to the Straits Settlements
  • 1905- married Ruth Shu-chiung Huang, whose sister was married to Lim Boon Keng, a physician who promoted social and educational reforms in Singapore. The sisters were daughters of Wong Nai Siong, a Chinese revolutionary leader and educator who had moved to the area from 1901 to 1906
  • 1907 - Moved to China, During his time in China, Wu's wife and two of their three sons died. While Ms Huang lived in Peking, Wu started a second family in Shanghai with Marie Lee Sukcheng, whom he had met in Manchuria. Wu had four children with Lee.
  • November 1931- Detained and interrogated by the Japanese authorities under suspicion of being a Chinese spy
